# AplusPrompt Brand Notes

## Product identity
AplusPrompt is an AI capability training platform for non-technical business users. It mostly starts with prompting, but the bigger promise is helping business people become confident AI users through interactive missions.

## Brand direction
Soft Japanese business-kawaii SaaS.

The app should feel:
- Cute, but professional
- Friendly, but credible
- Beginner-safe, but business-focused
- Like a guided training mission, not a video course
- Like a personal trainer / AI sensei, not a generic chatbot

## Mascot direction
Use the uploaded dog mascot images directly. Do not generate new mascots unless explicitly asked.

Mascot role:
- Smart dog coach / sensei / personal trainer
- Diagnoses mistakes
- Explains simply
- Encourages retry
- Rewards improvement

Suggested mascot states:
- coach_pointing_clipboard.png = teaching / mission intro
- coach_thumbs_up.png = encouragement / success
- coach_celebration.png = completion / XP reward
- coach_serious.png = serious feedback / needs improvement
- coach_thinking.png = analyzing / confused prompt
- coach_checklist.png = rubric / evaluation / next steps

## Visual style
- Warm ivory background, not pure white
- Deep forest green as primary brand color
- Sage green as secondary color
- Soft gold/orange for XP and rewards
- Rounded cards
- Soft shadows
- Clean SaaS dashboard layout
- Subtle Japanese-inspired details: paper texture, wave pattern, stamp-like badges, belt progression
- Friendly professional typography

## Recommended colors
- Background: #FAF7F1
- Primary green: #0F3D2E
- Sage green: #8E9A82
- Accent gold: #D9A441
- Soft orange: #F3B562
- Text: #222222
- Card background: #FFFFFF

## Avoid
- Do not make it look like a children’s game
- Do not make it look like a generic AI chatbot
- Do not make it look like a dark developer tool
- Do not use neon colors or overly gamer-style UI
