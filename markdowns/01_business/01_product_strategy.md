# Product Strategy — A+Prompt

## One-line definition

**A+Prompt is the AI gym for business owners: users learn AI by solving real business problems, doing prompt reps, getting form correction, and building supervised mini-agents.**

## The strategic wedge

The market is flooded with AI courses, prompt libraries, and bootcamps. A+Prompt is different because it is not content-first. It is **practice-first**.

Most prompt education says:

> Here is a good prompt. Copy it.

A+Prompt says:

> Write your prompt. Let us score it. Let us show what is missing. Now try again. Now compare the better output. Now save the workflow.

## Product category

A+Prompt sits between:

- AI education
- workflow automation
- small-business productivity
- agent-building tools
- business coaching

The category phrase for pitching:

> **AI upskilling through business simulation.**

Or more simply:

> **Duolingo for business AI usage, but with real work outputs.**

## Core thesis

AI does not automatically make users productive. The productivity lift depends on whether users can:

1. Define the task.
2. Provide useful context.
3. Specify sources and inputs.
4. Constrain the output.
5. Verify uncertainty.
6. Iterate.
7. Convert one-off prompts into repeatable workflows.

A+Prompt teaches this through interactive practice.

## Why now

Generative AI adoption is exploding, but usage quality is uneven. Business owners hear that AI can help with marketing, customer service, sales, and research, but their first experience often produces generic answers. The problem is not only model quality. It is the interaction skill gap.

## The AI K-curve framing

AI creates a K-curve:

- The upper curve: users who can use AI as a thinking partner, analyst, writer, workflow builder, and agent.
- The lower curve: users who only ask vague questions and get generic answers.

A+Prompt moves users from the lower curve to the upper curve.

- Family business operators
- Local service businesses
- Bootcamp attendees who still cannot apply AI at work

### Secondary user

- Chambers of commerce / business communities

- Corporate L&D teams for non-technical staff

## User pain

The user does not say “I need prompt engineering.”

They say:

- “AI gives me generic answers.”
- “I do not know what to ask.”
- “I tried ChatGPT but it was not useful.”
- “I do not have time to watch long courses.”
- “I want AI to help my actual business.”
- “I am scared of trusting wrong AI answers.”

## Product promise

> Learn AI by doing, not watching.

A+Prompt helps the user:

- Get useful AI outputs faster.
- Understand why prompts fail.
- Build confidence without technical jargon.
- Save reusable prompts for repeated business tasks.
- Safely graduate into supervised agent workflows.

## Business model summary

A+Prompt is designed as a freemium product. The first beginner path is free and ends with the **A+Prompt AI Able Certificate**. This free track moves users from AI Novice to AI Able through practical prompting fundamentals and gives them a visible completion reward.

The paid subscription starts after the certificate. Paid users unlock deeper business missions, full playbook memory, industry-specific context packs, advanced verification, and supervised agent-builder tracks such as the Customer Save Agent.

The product is not monetized as a one-time course. It is monetized as an ongoing AI training gym and business workflow companion.

See `01_business/04_business_model_and_certification.md` for the full model.

## Product moat over time

1. **Mission library** — business-specific scenarios across industries.
2. **Rubric data** — prompt attempts + scores + coach feedback become training data.
3. **Business playbooks** — users build personalized prompt/workflow libraries.
4. **Agent templates** — safe supervised mini-agents for customer service, marketing, research, operations.
5. **Industry context packs** — cafe, retail, salon, education, real estate, etc.

Why:

- Demo-friendly.
- Immediate ROI.
