# Pitch and Demo Script

## 15-second pitch

A+Prompt is the AI gym for business owners. Instead of watching prompt-engineering videos, users practice on real business missions, get prompts scored, receive form correction, earn a free AI Able Certificate, and upgrade into paid advanced tracks for supervised business agents.

## 45-second pitch

Small-business owners know AI is powerful, but many still use it like Google: short vague questions, no context, no verification, and no repeatable workflow. That creates generic answers and lost trust. A+Prompt fixes this by turning AI usage into guided practice. Users choose missions like customer complaint rescue, competitor analysis, or marketing campaigns. They write prompts, get scored, receive coaching, retry, compare improved outputs, and save the final prompt to their business playbook. The first level is free and ends with an AI Able Certificate. Advanced paid tracks teach business memory, verification, workflows, tool use, and safe supervised agents like the Customer Save Agent.

## 2-minute pitch

AI productivity gains are real, but they are uneven. Power users know how to frame tasks, provide context, specify output, verify uncertainty, and iterate. Novices often get left behind because they only ask vague questions and receive generic answers.

A+Prompt closes this AI usage gap. It is not a passive course or a prompt library. It is an AI training gym for business owners.

The user starts with a real business mission, such as saving an angry customer. They write their own prompt. A+Prompt scores the prompt using a business rubric: goal clarity, business context, source/input clarity, constraints, output format, verification, and actionability. Then an AI coach explains what is missing, rewrites the prompt, and shows how the improved prompt produces a better business output. The user practices again, saves their best prompt, and builds a reusable business playbook.

The final unlock is a supervised Customer Save Agent. It reads a complaint, identifies issue type and emotion, checks business policy, recommends an action, drafts a reply, and waits for human approval. This teaches the user the path from basic prompting to safe agent workflows.

## Demo flow

### Scene 1 — Landing page

Show tagline:

> A+Prompt — The AI Gym for Business Owners  
> Learn AI by doing, not watching.

> Start your first prompt workout

### Scene 2 — Mission selection

Show three mission cards:

1. Customer Save Agent
2. Competitor Analysis Gym
3. Marketing Campaign Gym

Choose **Customer Save Agent**.

### Scene 3 — Prompt attempt

Scenario:

> A customer says: “I ordered a cake for my daughter’s birthday and it arrived 2 hours late. The party was already over. I’m very disappointed.”

User prompt:

> Reply to this angry customer.

### Scene 4 — Score and coaching

Score: 38/100 — Needs Training

Feedback:

- Good: you identified the task.
- Missing: business context.
- Missing: refund/replacement policy.
- Missing: tone and length constraints.
- Missing: escalation rule.
- Missing: verification of risky promises.

Coach line:

> Your prompt has the right target, but weak form. The AI does not know what your business is allowed to offer, so it may overpromise.

### Scene 5 — Improved prompt

Show improved prompt:

> Act as a customer support coach for a bakery. A customer’s birthday cake arrived 2 hours late. Our policy allows either a 20% refund or free delivery voucher, but full refunds need manager approval. Write a warm apology under 120 words. Do not blame the driver. Recommend the safest offer and flag anything that needs manager approval.

### Scene 6 — Before/after comparison

Weak output:

- Generic apology.
- May promise full refund.
- No policy awareness.

Improved output:

- Warm apology.
- Policy-safe offer.
- Clear follow-up.
- Manager approval warning.

6. Create follow-up.
