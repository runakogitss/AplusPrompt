# Problem, User, Solution

## Problem statement

AI is creating a new productivity gap. People who know how to frame tasks, give context, verify outputs, and iterate with AI can turn generic chatbots into powerful business assistants. But non-technical users, especially busy small-business owners, often use AI like a search box: short prompts, vague context, no verification, and no repeatable workflow.

As a result, they get generic answers, lose trust in AI, and fail to capture productivity gains that more advanced users are already getting.

## Why this matters

Generative AI can create significant economic value, especially in functions like customer operations, marketing, sales, software engineering, and R&D. But the value does not arrive automatically just because someone has access to a chatbot. Users need interaction skills.

A+Prompt addresses the missing layer: practical AI usage training.

## Target user profile

### Persona 1 — Small business owner

**Name:** Lina  
**Business:** Bakery / cafe  
**Skill level:** Uses WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Sheets. Not technical.  
**Current AI behavior:** Types “make me promotion ideas” and gets generic output.  
**Pain:** Wants to use AI but does not know how to ask properly.  
**Desired outcome:** Better customer replies, marketing campaigns, and competitor ideas.

### Persona 2 — Family business operator

**Name:** Budi  
**Business:** Retail distributor  
**Skill level:** Business-savvy, tech-hesitant.  
**Current AI behavior:** Attended AI bootcamp but still cannot apply it daily.  
**Pain:** Needs AI workflows for supplier messages, price comparison, reports.  
**Desired outcome:** Repeatable AI playbook for common tasks.

### Persona 3 — SME consultant/trainer

**Name:** Maya  
**Work:** Teaches business owners AI basics.  
**Skill level:** Comfortable with AI.  
**Pain:** Hard to personalize practice for every learner.  
**Desired outcome:** Use A+Prompt as training infrastructure.

## User jobs to be done

1. When I have a business problem, help me turn it into a good AI task.
2. When my prompt is weak, explain why in simple terms.
3. When AI gives me generic output, show me how to improve it.
4. When I discover a useful prompt, save it so I can reuse it.
5. When I am ready, help me turn repeated tasks into safe supervised agents.

## Solution

A+Prompt is an interactive AI practice platform where users complete business missions. Each mission has:

- A real business scenario.
- A short coaching lesson.
- A prompt writing exercise.
- A scoring rubric.
- AI feedback.
- An improved prompt.
- Before/after output comparison.
- Saved prompt playbook.
- Optional agent-building unlock.

## Core transformation

Before A+Prompt:

> “AI is too generic. I do not know what to ask.”

After A+Prompt:

> “I can turn a business problem into a clear AI workflow, verify the result, and reuse it.”

## Why the gym metaphor works

Prompting is a skill. Like lifting weights, beginners have bad form. They need:

- Reps
- Form correction
- Progressive difficulty
- A coach
- Progress tracking
- Real goals

A+Prompt makes AI upskilling feel like training, not school.
